I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Iowa State University. I have accepted a position at the FHFA's Credit Risk Modelling Group to begin Summer 2020. My research deals with estimating the economic impacts of government policies on regional firm entry behavior, with secondary interest in econometric methods for regional economics.

This webpage hosts a variety of research information, working papers, academic information, and a list of references and guides I've found useful through my time as an Undergraduate, Graduate student, and now professional economist.

I am a senior economist at the FHFA's Credit Risk Modelling Group, where I focus on collateral and member credit risk modeling in the FHLBank System. My research deals with estimating the economic impacts of government policies on regional firm entry behavior, with secondary interest in econometric methods.

This webpage hosts a variety of research information, working papers, academic information, and a list of references and guides I've found useful through my time as an Undergraduate, Graduate student, and now professional economist.
